Oxygen Levels Critical

How to play

  • Click on the floor to move
  • Some doors can be locked by a switch next to it
  • Click on various terminals to use them
  • Click on people to chat; you might convince them to go to the cargo hold
  • Mouse wheel to zoom

How to win

  • Don't die
  • Be careful when opening cargo doors
  • Get to a sustainable level of oxygen consumption
  • Sacrifices may be required
